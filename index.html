Welcome to AUUM Vidyalaya

About Our School

AUUM Vidyalaya is a government school located in the heart of the city. We are committed to providing quality education to our students and helping them achieve their full potential. Our experienced faculty members and dedicated staff work together to create a supportive and engaging learning environment.

About Our Principal

Our principal, Ms. Smith, has been a dedicated educator for over 20 years. She holds a master's degree in education and has a passion for helping students achieve their full potential. She leads our school with a strong commitment to academic excellence and a focus on creating a safe and inclusive learning environment for all students.

Under her leadership, our school has implemented innovative programs that have increased student engagement and improved academic performance. Ms. Smith is a strong advocate for student success and works closely with teachers and staff to ensure that every student receives the support they need to thrive.

We are proud to have Ms. Smith as our principal and grateful for her unwavering commitment to our school community.

Managing Committee

Our school's managing committee is responsible for overseeing the school's operations and ensuring that the school is meeting the needs of our students and community. The committee is made up of dedicated volunteers who bring a diverse range of skills and expertise to the table.

Under the guidance of our committee chair, Mr. Johnson, the committee works closely with the principal and staff to develop policies and procedures that promote academic excellence, student welfare, and a safe and inclusive learning environment.

We are grateful for the hard work and dedication of our managing committee, who play a vital role in the success of our school.

Vice Principal

Our school's vice principal, Ms. Smith, is responsible for supporting the principal in overseeing the day-to-day operations of the school. Ms. Smith brings a wealth of experience to her role, having worked in education for over 20 years.

Ms. Smith is committed to ensuring that our students receive the best education possible and that they are prepared for success in college and beyond. She works closely with teachers and staff to develop innovative curricula and teaching methods that engage and challenge our students.

We are fortunate to have Ms. Smith as our vice principal and appreciate the dedication and hard work she brings to her role every day.
